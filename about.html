About the team behind The Reading Room

At The Reading Room, our team is made up of book lovers, discussion starters, and lifelong learners who believe that stories bring people together.

We're dedicated to creating a welcoming space where every reader can share ideas, discover new voices, and feel part of a vibrant community. From organizing monthly book circles to curating diverse reading lists, we work together to make reading not just a hobby — but a shared experience.

Agnes

Agnes started The Reading Room after realizing that her bookshelf was full of great stories she had no one to talk about.

With her love for planning and community, she turned quiet reading nights into friendly gatherings where every voice is heard.

Carolina

Caro believes every book is a doorway to another world. She curates the club's monthly reading themes, balancing classics with modern gems,

and never stops searching for hidden literary treasures. Her favorite part? Seeing how the same story sparks completely different emotions in everyone.

Frida

Frida brought The Reading Room to life online. With her creative flair and love for color, she designed the warm, welcoming look of the website

— a digital extension of the cozy corner where the group first met. She believes that good design can make reading feel even more magical.

Julia

Juls has the gift of conversation. She leads the group's discussions, asks thoughtful questions, and makes sure every reader feels included.

For her, The Reading Room isn't just about books — it's about the people who share them.
